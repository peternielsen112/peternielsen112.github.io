Chronicles of Wyuup: Rebellion

The land of Wyuup is engaged in a civil war. Two factions - the Revolution and the Resistance - fight for control of the government and the people. And Ren is stuck in the middle of it.

Kallwyn Jameson and his ragtag group of friends have defeated Quatremaine, the leader of the Directive of One, in a fierce battle; but now, they don't know who to trust. Now, under the direction of the Order of the Mask, they must infiltrate a castle and stop a dangerous alliance from becoming reality - before it's too late and Quatremaine gains power over the world.
