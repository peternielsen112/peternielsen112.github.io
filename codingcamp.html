Susanville Coding Camp

August 1-5 and 8-12, 2022

Susanville Coding Camp is a set of guided, in-person instructional lessons in computer programming for children ages 8 to 12 and will take place during the first two weeks of August. Sessions are 1.5 hours long and entail group activities, instruction, and question-and-answer.
